SUPPLY CHAIN TRACING USING BLOCKCHAIN

A journey from farms to your kitchen

Food supply chain management is the flow of agricultural products from farms, harvesters, processing, distributing, retail and finally consumers. It is a sequence that starts from food production and extends till final consumption. We aim to trace and authenticate food produced using blockchain technology.

Farming aspects


Crop Selection

Various crops are selected to plant, based on their demand in the market.

Fertilizing

A natural or synthetic material which is used to supply nutrients to growing plants.

Harvesting

Reaping plants that are ready to be harvested after maturity using various methods.

What happens in logistics?


Transport

Loads all the harvested produce and transports it to the storage facilities.

Storage

Responsible for the safe storage and proper maintenance of products.

Retail

Top-graded, standardized products are sold to end-consumer.

How it ends up on your table!


Tracing

The entire process from farms to kitchen is traced and displayed to the end-user.

Quality Assurance

Tracing mechanisms ensure the quality of the product on the plate.

Safety

Products are checked and re-checked to make sure they are safe.
